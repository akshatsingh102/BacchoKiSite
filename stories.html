📚 Amazing Stories Collection

Read these wonderful stories about brave heroes, wise gods, and magical adventures!

👑 Brave Kings and Heroes

Maharana Pratap

Maharana Pratap was a very brave king of Mewar. He loved his kingdom and his people very much. When a powerful emperor named Akbar wanted to take his kingdom, Maharana Pratap said "No!" and fought to protect his land.

What we learn: Always stand up for what is right, even when it's hard!

Ashoka the Great

Ashoka was a very powerful king of ancient India. After winning a big war, he felt very sad about all the fighting. He decided to follow the path of peace and kindness. He built many hospitals and schools for his people.

What we learn: Being kind and helping others is more important than being powerful!

King Porus vs Alexander

King Porus was a brave Indian king who fought against Alexander the Great. Even though Alexander had a bigger army, Porus fought bravely. When Alexander asked how he should be treated, Porus said "Like a king!" Alexander was so impressed that he gave Porus back his kingdom.

What we learn: True bravery and dignity are more valuable than winning!

🇮🇳 Bhagat Singh

Bhagat Singh was a young freedom fighter who loved India very much. He was very brave and fought against the British rulers to make India free. He was only 23 years old when he became a martyr for his country. He taught us that love for our country is the greatest thing.

What we learn: Love your country and stand up for freedom and justice!

🕉️ Divine Stories

Lord Shiva

Lord Shiva is known as the destroyer and transformer. He lives on Mount Kailash with his family. He is very kind and helps people who are in trouble. He has a third eye that can destroy evil, and he carries a trident. He loves to dance and is called Nataraja, the king of dance.

What we learn: Be kind to others and always help those in need!

Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna was born in Mathura and grew up in Vrindavan. He was very playful as a child and loved to play the flute. He protected the people from evil kings and taught everyone about love, duty, and righteousness. His favorite color is blue, and he loves butter!

What we learn: Be playful, loving, and always do what is right!

Hanuman Ji

Hanuman Ji is the greatest devotee of Lord Rama. He is very strong and can fly! He helped Lord Rama rescue Sita from the demon king Ravana. He is very humble and always ready to help others. He taught us that true strength comes from devotion and humility.

What we learn: Be humble, devoted, and always ready to help others!

Lord Rama

Lord Rama is known as Maryada Purushottam, the perfect man. He was a great king who always followed the path of righteousness. He went to the forest for 14 years to keep his father's promise. He fought the demon Ravana to rescue his wife Sita and taught us about duty, honor, and love.

What we learn: Always keep your promises and follow the path of righteousness!

Lord Ganesha

Lord Ganesha is the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom. He has an elephant head and a human body. He loves sweets, especially modaks! He is very wise and helps people solve their problems. He is always worshipped first before any important work.

What we learn: Be wise, patient, and always think before you act!

Tenali Rama

Tenali Rama was a very clever court jester in the court of King Krishnadevaraya. He was very funny and wise. He used his wit and humor to solve difficult problems and make everyone laugh. He taught us that intelligence and humor can solve many problems.

What we learn: Use your intelligence and humor to solve problems!

🧚‍♀️ Magical Fairy Tales

Cinderella

Cinderella was a kind and beautiful girl who lived with her mother and sisters. They were very mean to her, but she never lost hope. With the help of her fairy godmother, she went to the prince's ball. The prince fell in love with her, and they lived happily ever after.

What we learn: Be kind and never lose hope, good things will happen!

The Frog Prince

A beautiful princess dropped her golden ball into a well. A frog promised to get it back if she would be his friend. The princess agreed but didn't want to keep her promise. However, when she kissed the frog, he turned into a handsome prince!

What we learn: Always keep your promises, even if they seem difficult!

Rapunzel

Rapunzel was a beautiful girl with very long golden hair. She was locked in a tall tower by a witch. A prince heard her singing and climbed up her hair to meet her. They fell in love and escaped together, living happily ever after.

What we learn: Love and courage can overcome any obstacle!

Goldilocks and the Three Bears

Goldilocks was a curious little girl who found a house belonging to three bears. She tried their porridge, sat in their chairs, and slept in their beds. When the bears came home, they found her sleeping in Baby Bear's bed. She woke up and ran away, learning to respect others' things.

What we learn: Always respect other people's belongings and ask permission!

📖 हिंदी कहानियाँ (Hindi Stories)

ये सुंदर हिंदी कहानियाँ हैं जो आपको बहुत कुछ सिखाएंगी!

हाथी और चींटी (The Elephant and the Ant)

एक बार एक बहुत बड़ा हाथी था जो अपने आकार पर बहुत घमंड करता था। वह छोटे जानवरों को तंग करता रहता था। एक दिन एक छोटी सी चींटी ने उसे सबक सिखाने का फैसला किया।

चींटी ने हाथी के कान में घुसकर उसे बहुत परेशान किया। हाथी कितना भी बड़ा था, लेकिन छोटी चींटी के सामने वह बेबस हो गया। अंत में हाथी ने माफी मांगी और सभी जानवरों से दोस्ती कर ली।

सीख: आकार से कोई फर्क नहीं पड़ता, बुद्धि और साहस ही मायने रखता है!

लोमड़ी और अंगूर (The Fox and the Grapes)

एक भूखी लोमड़ी जंगल में घूम रही थी। तभी उसकी नज़र एक अंगूर के पेड़ पर पड़ी। पेड़ पर बहुत सुंदर और मीठे अंगूर लटक रहे थे। लोमड़ी ने अंगूर खाने की कोशिश की लेकिन वे बहुत ऊंचे थे।

लोमड़ी ने कूद-कूदकर अंगूर तोड़ने की बहुत कोशिश की, लेकिन वह असफल रही। अंत में थककर वह बोली, "ये अंगूर खट्टे हैं, मैं इन्हें नहीं खाना चाहती।" और वहां से चली गई।

सीख: जो चीज़ हमें नहीं मिलती, उसके बारे में बुरा कहना आसान है। लेकिन हमें हार नहीं माननी चाहिए!

खरगोश और कछुआ (The Rabbit and the Tortoise)

एक बार एक तेज़ दौड़ने वाला खरगोश और एक धीमा कछुआ थे। खरगोश हमेशा कछुए का मज़ाक उड़ाता था कि वह कितना धीमा है। एक दिन कछुए ने खरगोश को दौड़ का चुनौती दी।

दौड़ शुरू हुई। खरगोश तेज़ी से दौड़ा और कछुआ को पीछे छोड़ दिया। रास्ते में खरगोश को लगा कि वह जीत जाएगा, इसलिए वह एक पेड़ के नीचे सो गया। जब वह जागा तो कछुआ धीरे-धीरे चलकर जीत गया था।

सीख: धीमी गति से लगातार काम करना, तेज़ गति से रुक-रुक कर काम करने से बेहतर है!

भेड़िया और भेड़ (The Wolf and the Sheep)

एक बार एक चालाक भेड़िया था जो भेड़ों को खाना चाहता था। लेकिन चरवाहा हमेशा सतर्क रहता था। एक दिन भेड़िए ने एक चाल चली। वह भेड़ की खाल पहनकर भेड़ों के झुंड में घुस गया।

भेड़िए ने सोचा कि वह भेड़ों को आसानी से खा सकेगा। लेकिन चरवाहे ने उसे पहचान लिया क्योंकि वह बहुत अलग तरीके से चल रहा था। चरवाहे ने भेड़िए को भगा दिया और भेड़ों को बचा लिया।

सीख: झूठ और धोखा कभी लंबे समय तक नहीं चलता। सच्चाई हमेशा सामने आ जाती है!

चील और कबूतर (The Eagle and the Pigeon)

एक बार एक शक्तिशाली चील थी जो अपनी ताकत पर बहुत घमंड करती थी। वह छोटे पक्षियों को डराती रहती थी। एक दिन एक छोटा सा कबूतर उसके सामने आया और बोला, "आप बहुत ताकतवर हैं, लेकिन क्या आप मेरे साथ दोस्ती कर सकते हैं?"

चील ने कबूतर की बात सुनी और सोचा कि दोस्ती करना भी अच्छा है। कबूतर ने चील को सिखाया कि ताकत का सही उपयोग कैसे करना है। अब चील सभी पक्षियों की रक्षा करती है और सब उसे प्यार करते हैं।

सीख: ताकत का उपयोग दूसरों को डराने के लिए नहीं, बल्कि उनकी मदद करने के लिए करना चाहिए!
