🎨 Drawing Tips:

  • Click and drag to draw with the pencil tool
  • Use the Fill tool to color inside shapes - click inside any closed area!
  • The Fill tool only fills the area you click, respecting boundaries
  • Use the Eraser tool to remove parts of your drawing
  • Choose different colors from the color picker
  • Adjust brush size with the slider (1-30)
  • Click "Clear Drawing" to start over
  • Works on touch devices too!
