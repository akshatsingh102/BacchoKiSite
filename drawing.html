🎨 Artist Tips:

🖌️ Drawing Tips

  • Choose from Brushes: Pencil, Paint Brush, Marker, or Crayon
  • Try different colors from the palette or use custom color picker
  • Adjust brush size (1-50 pixels) and opacity (10-100%)
  • Use smooth drawing mode for flowing curves

📐 Shape Tools

  • Click shapes (Lines, Circles, Rectangles, etc.) to draw perfect shapes
  • Click and drag to make shapes bigger or smaller
  • Try the Star and Heart shapes for fun!
  • Shapes respect your current color and brush size

🎨 Special Tools

  • Fill Bucket: Click inside closed shapes to fill them
  • Spray Paint: Creates realistic spray-paint effect
  • Eraser: Remove parts of your drawing
  • Text Tool: Add text to your artwork

📱 Mobile Tips

  • Works great on tablets and phones!
  • Touch and drag to draw
  • Big buttons for easy tool switching
  • Canvas automatically adjusts to your screen size
