📚 Fun Color Facts

🌈 Rainbow Colors

Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet - remember ROY G BIV!

🎨 Primary Colors

Red, Blue, and Yellow are primary colors. You can't make them by mixing other colors!

🟢 Nature's Colors

Green is the most common color in nature because of all the plants and trees!

🌈 Color Learning Tips:

  • Click on any color to hear its name and learn about it
  • Try the color mixer to see how colors combine
  • Play the color games to test your knowledge
  • Look around you and name the colors you see
  • Practice mixing colors with paints or crayons
  • Remember that colors can make us feel happy, calm, or excited!
