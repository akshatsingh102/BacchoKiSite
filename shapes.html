🔷 Shape Learning Tips:

  • Click on any shape card to select it
  • Click anywhere on the canvas to create the selected shape
  • Click on a shape to select it (you'll see a dotted outline)
  • Drag shapes around the canvas to move them
  • Drag the red square handle to resize shapes
  • Click "Delete Shape" to remove the selected shape
  • Double-click on any shape to quickly delete it
  • Choose different colors to make your shapes beautiful
  • Create multiple shapes and arrange them as you like
  • Click "Say Shape" to hear the shape name
  • Click "Stop" to stop any ongoing speech
  • Click "Clear Canvas" to remove all shapes
  • Look for these shapes in the world around you!
